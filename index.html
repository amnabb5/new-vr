About Me

I’m a 3D artist focused on creating clean, impactful visual experiences and product presentations. I use Blender to build realistic scenes, lighting, and compositions that bring ideas to life.

I also have a background in frontend development and UI/UX design, using tools like Figma, React, and Illustrator when needed — helping me bridge the gap between visuals, design, and interactive experiences.

I care about detail, composition, and creating visuals that not only look good but communicate clearly.
